§01Approach

Systems that don't fail quietly.

My instinct is Domain-Driven Design, tested to the seams, shipped through continuous delivery. I've carried this method across healthcare, finance, gaming and IoT — five industries, one discipline.

The best architecture is the one the team can still change six months later. I optimise for clarity over cleverness, small safe deployments over heroic releases, and documented decisions over tribal memory.

Bounded contexts, clean seams. Every service independently deployable and independently understood.

02

03
